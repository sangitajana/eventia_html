ABOUT US

Organization, Details, logistics

Three words that can spoil any event chair’s important day. At Chappelow Events, we help manage your big ideas and your smallest concerns to ensure you are stress-free and enjoying every moment. For more than eight years, we’ve been working with clients to create exceptional experiences for their nonprofit fundraiser or social gathering. As a boutique company, we’re driven by a childlike excitement for what we do and a love of collaboration with our clients. It’s our goal to leave you and your guests with an event that exceeds expectations and lasts in your memories long after the last toast.
